### Why No Validator Rewards Pool?

**Research shows ALL successful L1s skip validator pools:**
- **Cosmos Hub**: 0% pool, ~19% APY from inflation only
- **Polkadot**: 0% pool, ~14% APY from inflation
- **Avalanche**: 0% pool, ~9% APY, **$290M ecosystem fund instead**
- **Solana**: 0% pool, ~7% APY from inflation
- **Near Protocol**: 0% pool, **$800M ecosystem fund**
- **Polygon**: 0% pool, **$1B ecosystem fund**

**Sultan L1 validators get 26.67% APY from inflation (hardcoded in staking.rs):**
- 8% annual inflation = 40M SLTN/year
- ~2.54 SLTN per block reward
- Competitive with Cosmos (19%) and better than Polkadot (14%)
- **Automatic, sustainable, no pool needed**

**Benefits of eliminating the pool:**
1. ‚úÖ Simpler investor narrative
2. ‚úÖ More capital for ecosystem growth (like Avalanche, Near, Polygon)
3. ‚úÖ No appearance of validator enrichment scheme
4. ‚úÖ Follows proven success patterns
5. ‚úÖ Inflation is enough - pool is redundant
